Vegan Kale Smoothie

Description

Mix all the ingredients in a blender

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup coconut water
  • 1 medium orange (can also use ¼ cup orange juice or 2-3 clementines)
  • 1 cup kale, packed
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon agave
  • 1 frozen banana

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all the ingredients.
  2. Blend until well combined.
  3. *If using a fresh banana, add ice if desired.
